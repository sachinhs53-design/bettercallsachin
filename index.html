About

An advocate
trusted in court.

Sachin Kumar Suman is a practising Advocate enrolled with the Bar Council of Delhi, appearing before the District Courts of Delhi, the Delhi High Court, and in Arbitration proceedings.

His practice focuses on commercial and civil disputes, cheque bounce matters under the Negotiable Instruments Act, and arbitration-related issues — representing both businesses and individuals with the same rigour and commitment regardless of the size of the matter.

Every client deserves honest advice, clear strategy, and a lawyer who fights for them — not just in the courtroom, but at every step of the way.

Alongside practice, Sachin writes plain-language articles on Indian law, making complex legal concepts accessible to people who need to understand their rights without wading through legal jargon.
